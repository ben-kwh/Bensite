Energy and Sustainability Specialist

Focus on Energy (CB&I)

Feb 2013 – May 2018 Madison, WI

CB&I acquired the Shaw Group in 2013, and continued serving as the Program Administrator for Focus on Energy. CB&I was renewed for an additional 4 year contract for the period of 2015-2018. During this tenure, our needs for major reporting and data overhauls at Focus on Energy became apparent. We had just figured out how get processes to make sure data entered the enterprise tracking system, but we had very little data coming out. How do you run a program when you don’t know if you’re winning or losing? This period was pivotal for me personally too. It became very apparent we needed better tools, because multiple projects we were working on were crashing excel. We needed to learn how to handle big data, and launched a new track of learning for me and my staff: python, SQL, AWS.

Highlights:

  • Wrote an amended contract and goal structure with WPSC. This new structure unified a multi-goal contract into a singular MMBtu savings goal and corrected a misaligned incentive structure that was diverging our clients needs with our own.
  • Developed a real-time Cost-Effectiveness workbook used across all program in our portfolio. Cost-effectiveness in utility programs involves many speculations beyond just current performance like install rates and the future value of energy. Although this is typically the most important metric to a program, it is usually not calculated until well after a program year is complete. Cutting the processing time of this metric, allowed us to literally make process changes quarters and months quicker than before.
  • Created and propagated a critical Cost-of-Acquisition metric into all data. Imagine not knowing how cost-per-widget varied between factories, this was the equivalent of our programs. This enabled us to level the playing field on how we compared programs against each other or how they tracked historically.
